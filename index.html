Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about Pactora? Here are some answers to help you understand our service better.

  • How does Pactora personalize my shopping experience?

    Pactora uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your preferences. It asks simple questions to understand your needs, making sure every recommendation is tailored just for you.

  • Is Pactora free to use?

    Pactora is free to trial to ensure you love the experience. We offer a premium subscription priced at $9.99 per year that gives you access to your personalized shopper on mobile and web.

  • How does Pactora ensure unbiased product recommendations?

    Our AI algorithm is designed to be unbiased. It does not favor any brand or product. Recommendations are based solely on matching products to your specific needs and preferences.

  • Can I return products recommended by Pactora?

    Returns and refunds are subject to the policies of the store where you purchase the product. Pactora guides you to the right product but the purchase transaction is between you and the store.

  • Does Pactora store my personal data?

    We value your privacy. Pactora stores minimal data required to improve your shopping experience and adheres to strict data protection and privacy policies.
